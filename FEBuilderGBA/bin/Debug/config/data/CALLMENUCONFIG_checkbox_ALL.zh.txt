01=Not target moved units	It will only work on units that have not yet acted.\r\nIt does not work on units that have already acted and are grayed out.
02=Called unit will be moved(gray)	The unit you called in is now active.\r\nIf all units have been called in, the turn is effectively over.
03=Called unit will be half HP	Halves the HP of the unit you have called.\r\nIt will not drop below 1 HP, but if the unit is in a poisoned state, be aware of the poison damage on the next turn.
