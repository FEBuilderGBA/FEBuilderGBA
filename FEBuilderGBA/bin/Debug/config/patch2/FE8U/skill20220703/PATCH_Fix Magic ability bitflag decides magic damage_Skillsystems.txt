NAME=Fix: Magic ability bitflag decides magic damage(Skillsystems)
INFO=The strength magic split used to determine if a weapon should do magic damage based on the weapon's type. For e\xample, an anima tome would always do magic damage, even if the magic ability bitflag (0x2) was off.\r\n\r\nThe new change makes it so you can decide if weapons do magic damage by using the magic ability bitflag

IF:0x0800=0x0 0xC0 0x9F 0xE5 0x1C 0xFF 0x2F 0xE1


TYPE=ADDR
TAG=#ENGINE

ADDRESS=$GREP4ENDA+0 0x5A 0x20 0x25 0x52 0x14 0x27 0x4C 0x20 0x20 0x58 0x40 0x25 0x05 0x42 0x05 0xD1
